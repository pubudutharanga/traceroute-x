<h2>🚀 What is Google Antigravity?</h2>
<p>Google Antigravity introduces a fresh approach to Visual Studio Code by enabling agentic development. Currently available for free during its public preview, it leverages the capabilities of Google's Gemini 3 AI models, alongside Claude Opus 4.5 and Claude Sonnet 4.5.</p>

<p>Consider the traditional workflow: with familiar coding assistants like GitHub Copilot, you typically write the core code while the AI suggests supplementary lines. The heavy lifting remains largely in your hands.</p>

<div class="highlight-box">
    <p><strong>With Antigravity, you can assign comprehensive tasks to AI agents.</strong> They thoughtfully plan the approach, draft the code, run tests, and present the outcomes for your review. This subtle shift allows you to move closer to the role of an engineering manager.</p>
</div>

<p>We are gently transitioning from Generation 1 AI tools—focused on autocomplete and suggestions—to Generation 2, featuring autonomous agents that can operate more independently. This represents a meaningful transformation in our everyday workflow.</p>

<h3>The Mission Control Experience</h3>
<p>Opening Antigravity presents a balanced split-screen interface. On one side, you have your trusted code editor; on the other, the Agent Manager, where you can thoughtfully orchestrate multiple AI agents handling different tasks simultaneously.</p>

<p>Increasingly, developers are finding value in spending a portion of their time in the Agent Manager, thoughtfully reviewing the accomplishments of agents while they focus on broader priorities.</p>

<h3>What Makes Antigravity Different</h3>
<p>A notable distinction is in the outputs Antigravity generates. Rather than just offering conversational responses, it focuses on producing verifiable, structured artifacts:</p>

- **Implementation Plans** that gently outline the steps needed for completion
- **Task Lists** breaking down complex endeavors into manageable pieces
- **Code Diffs** elegantly highlighting exact modifications
- **Screenshots and Browser Recordings** visually validating UI functionality
- **Test Results** providing confidence that everything operates smoothly

<p>This level of transparency fosters trust, making it beautifully simple to verify what the AI agents have achieved.</p>

<h3>What Can These Agents Actually Do?</h3>
<p>Antigravity agents are designed to gracefully handle tasks that previously required extensive manual effort:</p>

<p><strong>They can navigate the web:</strong> Set an agent to open a browser, interact with your local site, gracefully click through flows, and thoughtfully verify outcomes.</p>

<p><strong>They coordinate across files:</strong> Rather than viewing files in isolation, agents can orchestrate updates across dozens of interconnected files harmoniously.</p>

<p><strong>They utilize the command line:</strong> Agents are capable of running terminal commands, seamlessly installing packages, and configuring your environment.</p>

<p><strong>They grasp project context:</strong> By gently reading through your codebase, the agent builds a holistic understanding of how your architecture weaves together.</p>
